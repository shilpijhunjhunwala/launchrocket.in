An instant audit against every mandatory declaration

Upload your current label and we map it against the Legal Metrology (Packaged Commodities) Rules and platform requirements for your exact category — flagging what's missing, wrong, or positioned incorrectly before a marketplace bot ever sees it.

  • MRP (inclusive of all taxes), net quantity in metric units, and unit sale price where required.
  • Importer / manufacturer name & address and country of origin.
  • Manufacturing / import & expiry dates, batch and consumer-care contact.
  • Category marks — FSSAI logo & licence, BIS/ISI, care labelling and more.
