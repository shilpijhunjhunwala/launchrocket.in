TariffProof · Trade & Duty Intelligence

One code decides everything

Every import and export hinges on one 8-digit HS code — it sets your customs duty, your GST, the licences you trigger and your penalty risk. Get it wrong and you overpay in silence, or underpay and answer for it later. TariffProof gets the code right, shows the full duty stack, finds FTA savings, and flags every clearance — with a reasoning trail on each SKU.
