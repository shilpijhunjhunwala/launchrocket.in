Export Type Approvals

The certifications that gate connected products

Wireless and electronic products need country-specific type approval before they can legally be sold. We manage lab testing, the technical file and filing — and reuse test reports across markets to cut cost.

United States

FCC Authorization

Mandatory for electronic and wireless products entering the US — Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and other RF devices. We coordinate accredited lab testing, prepare the technical file, and manage FCC certification (or SDoC where applicable) end to end.

FCC Part 15FCC IDSDoCWireless / RF
European Union

CE / RED Marking

The CE mark is your passport to the 27-country EU market. We manage conformity assessment across the applicable directives — Radio Equipment (RED), EMC and Low Voltage (LVD) — compile the Declaration of Conformity and get your product market-ready.

CE MarkingREDEMCLVD
Canada

ISED Certification

Canada's ISED (formerly Industry Canada) approval under the RSS standards runs closely parallel to FCC — so we sequence the two to share test data and minimise duplicate cost.

ISEDRSSIC ID
Brazil & beyond

ANATEL & Multi-Country

ANATEL homologation for Brazil, plus a coordinated EMC/CB-Scheme strategy for Australia, Japan and other markets — one test programme, many destinations.
