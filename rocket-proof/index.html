The RocketProof Trust Charter

The rules that protect you — and why marketplaces and serious brands work with us.
  • We never buy, sell or seed reviews on marketplaces — only Vine and genuine purchasers.
  • No review gating: sampled reviews publish at every star level. That's what makes them credible.
  • Every sampled review is disclosure-tagged and written by a KYC-verified human.
  • No one edits a review except its author; full moderation audit trail per IS 19000:2022.
  • AI screens every submission for fraud — we police our own panel harder than anyone.
  • If a partner asks us to break these rules, we decline the engagement.
