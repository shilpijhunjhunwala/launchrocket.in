Enabling Global Market Access

India to the World.
The World to India.

Launch Rocket enables two-way commerce: Indian manufacturers and brands access markets across South-East Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas — while global brands gain structured, compliant access to India’s vast omnichannel retail opportunity.

  • Outbound: Enabling Indian Exports

    Shipping bills, certificates of origin, RCMC, APEDA/FSSAI clearances, and customs classification — full documentation and compliance for Indian brands going global.

  • Inbound: Enabling India Market Entry

    Import licensing, BIS/FSSAI/CDSCO registrations, HS code classification, customs duty advisory, and omnichannel retail onboarding for global brands entering India.

  • Cross-Border E-Commerce & Retail

    Managed accounts on Amazon, Flipkart, Noon, Shopee, Lazada, and other platforms — for Indian sellers expanding outward and global brands landing in India.
