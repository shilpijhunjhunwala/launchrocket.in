Navigate India's Regulatory Landscape

India's certification framework spans electronics, food, medical devices, packaging, labelling, and waste management — mandatory for every importer, brand, and marketplace seller. We manage the full process end-to-end so your products can be listed and sold without interruption.

BIS

BIS / CRS Certification

Mandatory for 400+ product categories. ISI Mark, CRS for electronics, and Scheme X for foreign manufacturers importing into India.

FSSAI Registration & License

Mandatory for all food businesses, importers, and marketplace sellers. Central, State, and Basic licensing including import NOC and labelling compliance.

CDSCO — Medical Devices & Cosmetics

Class A–D medical device registration, cosmetics license, pharmaceuticals, and import license. Mandatory for import and sale in India.
